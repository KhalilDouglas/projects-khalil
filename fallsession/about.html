About O&M

At O&M , we believe skincare should be natural, effective, and sustainable. Our products are handcrafted with love using 100% plant-based ingredients.

We started O&M in 2025 with a simple mission: create lotions and oils that nourish your skin while caring for the planet. From lavender lotion to coconut oil, each product is carefully made in small batches for the best quality.

We’re proud to be cruelty-free, eco-friendly, and dedicated to bringing you skincare that makes you feel confident and radiant.
